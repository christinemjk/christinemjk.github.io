People are multifaceted. Shouldn't your style reflect that?

Rock

I know that some days, I want to dress like a total badass, "Hey, I'm headed skydiving. Yeah, I'm jumping out of a plane." Other days, hipster-grunge, anyone else hitting up that warehouse party in Brooklyn?

Rose

Back home in Annapolis, going out on the boat, prep me out. Headed to the office for a meeting, classy and corporate with feminine touches, gold jewelry, and a blazer.

Rock x Rose

It's not even always the place you're headed, but the mood you're in. Some days, I just want to brood and wear all black graphic tees, some days I want to smile and shine like the sun in all sorts of colors. The point is, rarely are people just one type of look. I wanted a brand that supports and represents that. Don't feel stuck in one style because you're scared you can't "pull off" that look.

Soul
